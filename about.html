About DevHub

Why Choose Us

DevHub is a innovative platform that aims to bring developers and businesses in contact with each other to create the perfect match. It helps developers to find job opportunities and it tracks the perfect candidate for businesses. Perfect if you're looking for a job or a future employee.

What You Get

There are a lot of benefits of being a member of the DevHub family. You will get to connect with a huge network of developpers and businesses and expand your network worldwide. Also, you will find opportunities regarding your interests and whishes and maybe more!

Meet The Energy

DevHubs mission is to improve and to keep it up to date. Its goal is to always develop in order to get better and fullfill its users recommondations.




Meet the team

Let us introduce us! We, Gijs, Kyle and Babette are the founders of this amazing initiative. As we are future developers and would really like to work and develop ourselves in this field, we decided it would be a great idea to establish a network of developpers and businesses with work opportunities in order to realise our dream.
